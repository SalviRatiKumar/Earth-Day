EARTH DAY


Earth Day is an Annual event celebrated around the world the on April 22 to
demonstrate support for environmental protection. The first Earth Day Celebration
took place on March 21, 1970, the Vernal equinox that year.

Earth Day is a name used for two similar global observances.While some people
celebrate Earth Day around the time of the March Equinox, others observe the
occasion on April 22 each year.
